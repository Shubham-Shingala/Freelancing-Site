For Buyer

Step 1
Post a Project
In this Step 1, Buyer Just Need to Post a Project to hire some freelancer with all necessary details like budget, category, duration of the project.
Step 2
Hire Freelancer
In this Step 2, Buyer Can hire freelancer after seeing all the bids by different Freelancers. They both can interact with eachother regarding their work requirements and all.
Step 3
Project Completion
In this Step 3, Freelancer will deliver the final project to buyer and if buyer satisfied with the work, then buyer can complete the work.

For Freelancers

Step 1
Choose a Project
In this Step 1, Freelancer will have to choose the project, which he / she wants to do, and can complete it within specified timeline.
Step 2
Take the Project
In this Step 2, Freelancer will have to interact with buyer and decide all the requirements and if everything is perfect then buyer will give project to buyer.
Step 3
Deliver Project
In this Step 3, Freelancer have to deliver the work to buyer as per the working details.
